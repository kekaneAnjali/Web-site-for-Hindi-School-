Registration fees $150/ year ( Classes meet every third and first Sunday beginning September 17th and ending May 20th. Meeting times are 10:30a.m to 12:00p.m) Sibling fee will be $75/year. Please make cheques payable to Hindu Temple of Wisconsin. For more information please contact any one of the Volunteer Teacher.

There are 3 levels of Hindi Classes, and the course covered is as follows:

Beginners –

Mastery of all Vowels and transformation of vowels, consonants names of some common colors, fruits, vegetables counting numbers, animals.

Intermediate level –

Two , three and four letter words without matras and involving one matra at a time

Advanced level –

Reading words involving mixed matras and reading passages Writing sentences using all type of words In addition to Hindi vocabulary development, poems (action songs)/moral stories, group conversations, and games are conducted in the respective groups. Registrations can be done at temple office and textbooks can be bought on the first day of class.
